# Formal Analytic Rubric - ISS Greenhouse Module

Use when a four-level portfolio, research, or graduate-program artifact is needed.

| Criterion | 4 - Accomplished | 3 - Proficient | 2 - Developing | 1 - Beginning |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Evidence selection | Selects the most relevant evidence and distinguishes symptom from cause | Selects relevant evidence with minor omissions | Includes mixed relevant/irrelevant evidence | Provides little usable evidence |
| Source diversity | Integrates three or four source types | Uses at least two source types | Relies mostly on one source | Does not identify sources clearly |
| Mechanistic explanation | Accurately explains microgravity, statolith direction, and gravitropic response with appropriate qualification | Explains the central mechanism accurately | Names the mechanism but leaves important gaps | Gives an unsupported or inaccurate mechanism |
| Alternative evaluation | Rejects a plausible alternative using specific contradictory evidence | Rejects an alternative with generally relevant evidence | Rejects an alternative with weak support | Does not evaluate an alternative |
| Engineering reasoning | Defines a feasible cue, criterion, and constraint tied to the problem | Provides a relevant solution with criterion or constraint | Provides a loosely related solution | Solution does not address orientation |
| Communication | Clear, concise, accurate vocabulary, complete | Mostly clear and complete | Meaning is understandable but inconsistent | Incomplete or difficult to interpret |
