Lesson purpose

TEACHER QUICK START

Students investigate a plant-orientation failure in microgravity, distinguish symptoms from causes, and propose a feasible directional cue.

Correct diagnosis Missing or altered gravity cues disrupt normal root and shoot orientation responses.

Watch for

MISCONCEPTIONS
Do not say:
Roots become completely random in space.
Emphasize:
Plants use several interacting signals; the case isolates one important directional cue.
